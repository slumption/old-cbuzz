Brighton's Desirable Bars...

The following bars come highly recommended by the cBuzz crew. This may be to do with the fact that they have paid us large sums of money!! More realistically the information contained here reflects the good naturedness of the landlord/lady and the length of time that they will put up with our tab not being paid off. If you want to get information up here about your bar then contact cBuzz with an approximate figure of how high you'ld let the tab get before sending the heavies around.
