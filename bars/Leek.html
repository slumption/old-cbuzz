The Leek and Winkle is @ 39 Ditchling Road, Brighton

How to get there: Walk to the Level and it's on the Ditchling Road side of the playing fields, opposite the 5 aside pitch.

Bloke down the pubs comments : Always busy, especially on a Friday and Saturday night.

There's always a good mix of people at the Leek, and it has always been a very popular student bar, so if you've not been there yet, you should.
