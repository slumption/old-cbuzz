What is the GinZing gallery?

The GinZing gallery is you're chance to be seen on the internet and win a case of GinZing. Each month we will be displaying a photo of the GinZing Drinker of the month who will win a crate - all you have to do is send your photos of yourselves drinking GinZing and the best gets the crate and an appearance in the gallery. Simple.
