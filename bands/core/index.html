biography

Core are a four-piece Brighton (uk) based band whose sound and vision is clearly heading towards the next millenium.

Combining irresistible melodies with swirling guitar scapes and pulsing backbeats, Core are on a fresh and exciting tip.

Formed in 1994 Core have toured across Europe, North America and the UK. Having gigged at over 200 venues the band have gained vital experience, as well as building an extensive fan base mailing list in excess of 500, that has set the foundations for a future that is very much there for the taking.

The past six months have seen Core concentrating solely on songwriting. The result of this endeavour has led to a 3-song sampler recording (available on request). Featuring the positive energies of "I Know It" and "Repeat" and the challenging lyrics on "Change the Record", these tracks represent the bands current favourite numbers.

Core are a band more than capable of cutting their own commercial niché in todays worldwide market.
