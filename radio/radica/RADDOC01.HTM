RADICA FOR AUDIO PROCESSING

RADICA BROADCAST SYSTEMS LIMITED
recommends Inovonics Inc.
Audio Processing Equipment.

We supply broadcast audio processing equipment to both BBC and Independent stations in the UK, and to other stations world-wide, primarily from Inovonics Inc of Santa Cruz California USA. We are their UK and Republic of Ireland distributor and we like their sound!

Quite often stations want to make their sound distinctive and Inovonics products such as the Type 250-00 FM multi-band processor allow this without over-complex digital (which often means quazi-digital but we'll put the price up anyway!) front-ends as promoted by some other companies. European ears are different from those of our American cousins, so Radica offers a number of modification upgrades to standard units. For example we have developed the Type 250-00 FM GFI designed for UK commercial use, often with the Type 716-00 'David II' combined digital over-shoot compensated stereo encoder and final protection limiter. This gives results similar to those from well known alternatives - but at a more cost effective price and with less of the unwanted 'digital clipping' sounds we've come to know all too well.
