About Justice?

Justice? - the people who squatted the Courthouse and brought you the Squatter's Estate Agency. The collective who've been arrested more times than they've had hot dinners. It's an identity for a collective of people who are disgusted by things like the powers of the state and the destruction of the environment; it's a resource to make things better. It's not a clique. Nobody is a "member", you won't be given a membership form to fill in, and nobody tells you what to do. Well, they do, but when they've finished ranting, we ignore them anyway. There aren't any leaders.

Wanna help with SchNEWS but don't live in Brighton? Fear not! You can still help...
