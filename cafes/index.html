Brighton's Cafe Culture

Brighton, as any visitor, resident and Raj will know, has a thriving cafe culture. Many a day can be spent trekking from cafe to cafe - consuming enough coffee to party energetically throughout the night. We at cBuzz endeavour to spend as much time as we possibly can socialising in this unique environment; and therefore much of the business side of cBuzz takes place in many of what we regard as the better ones. So as a result we present here to you the undisputable guide to the hippest, happenin' cafes.
