What's this all about?

1/6/96

old.cbuzz.co.uk Right listen up, I've noticed my ride guide getting bigger and there's a few thing's I need to point out.

If an article is called new route, it's new route. If it's called rant 'n' ride it's a new route and my points on what ever the latest gossip is.

If my descriptions are a bit crap it's because I have to try and remember the trail, as soon as I get It together and take some good photo's this won't be a problem.

I assume when I write that riders who read this are locals so if I talk of any places you don't know of you'll have to ask someone for some directions. I know this is a bit crap, but this isn't intended to be a national ride guide, it's just places that are worth riding if your in the area so you may need a OS map of Brighton if you don't know it well.

Please respect the routes I write about and don't FUCK THEM UP by locking your wheels up and trashing the trail or be clever and try to knock people over. It has taken me a year and a half of riding with a lot of good local riders to gain their trust and be shown some of these trails and others are my own discovery. Don't mess things up, I'm not saying only "me and my mates" can ride here, I'm just saying a lot of these routes are on land that could easily be put out of limits to us through a moments stupidity.
