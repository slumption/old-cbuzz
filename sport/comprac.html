Scott Comp Racing - cost - 900.

The only cromo framed race bike. This was the only rigid bike Scott gave us to test. Full cromo frame and forks made fro Scotts new ultra light tubing. LX/XT components. Avid levers, mavic 221 rims and ritchey tires. Light frame and wheels, overall good x/c bike. The Scott bars were to narrow and messed the handling up. Overall it was a really good x/c bike and I wouldn't feel uncomfy racing it. For the price of a par of Judys and new bars you could have a Team Racing with Judys and an Alu frame.

Overall: The Scott comp racing was the best bike on test for x/c. A Light bike good component spec. The wheels and groupset were excllent and we had no problems with them.

Down side: The scott vertigo LSD was a good all rounder but needed the stem and tires changing it also needs wider bars.
